This is in part due to our unique staffing model. While we keep a "core team" of engineers who have been with the project continuously since our first line of code, we also employ a global team of specialists who can make meaningful contributions to specific areas of the application based on their unique abilities. The result is a blend of institutional knowledge and continuity mixed with deep capabilities.
