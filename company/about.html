Activecell is the small business management platform.

The product is software offered as a service that helps small businesses analyze historical accounting data and build forward-looking financial models.

How does it work?

Once you create your account, you can connect Activecell to your QuickBooks or Xero account so that the system can retrieve historical data. No need to worry: the system is read-only and does not send changes to your source data.

Then, our planning model allows you to ditch your old spreadsheets and plan your business online. That means no more ridiculous spreadsheet models standing apart from your accounting data. No more manual data entry. Just drag the chart values to update your plan. Compare plan to actuals, test the impact of complex scenarios in seconds, and share the plan with others.

What do I get?

We offer dead simple reporting for all your stakeholders. With just a few clicks, put together reports for yourself, your employees, your investors, whomever you like. The reports are just as easy to download to edit, or ship in pdf format via email right from the platform.

Our dashboards allow you to prioritize the metrics most important to your business, and with a library of over 100 analyses close at hand, you will have no trouble hand-picking the business drivers that mean the most to you. These metrics show up front and center every time you log on, updated automatically.

Is this software or consulting?

The product is software and does not a consulting arrangement, though our expert network is a great place to start if you are looking for an expert to help you get started.

The product is offered in a software-as-a-service model, which means that businesses can pay for the software on a monthly or annual basis, and they can instantly access the software (and its continuous updates and enhancements) online.

The product is also offered in a "white label" arrangement. If you have clients or users that would benefit from the platform, but you don't want to send them to Activecell because you want to retain the relationship, this is no problem at all! We have a branded solution that allows you to use your website address, logo, and color scheme (and more advanced branding if you like!). This solution is ideal for accountants, bookkeepers, and business consultants, as well as technology companies that don't want to build out the data connectivity and charting for small business applications.
