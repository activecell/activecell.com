Activecell product information

Activecell is the small business management platform. With Activecell, a small business owner can quickly and easily build a dynamic financial plan that connects to their accounting data for historical analysis.

  • Activecell is Software-as-a-Service
  • Plans start at $49.99/month
  • Product targets small businesses (fewer than 100 employees), including startups
  • Data integration is currently possible with QuickBooks Desktop, QuickBooks Online, and Xero accounting suites
  • Branded (white label) solution allows accountants/bookkeepers/consultants to offer Activecell to their clients with their own branding
  • Activecell is owned and built by Profitably, Inc.

Profitably, Inc. company information

  • Founded: March 2010 in New York City
  • Funding: $300k seed in 2010
  • Funding: $1.1M Series A in 2011
  • Original product launched under the Profitably brand
  • Complete product rebuild initiated in April 2012 to relaunch as Activecell
